Website Style Guide

The look that I am trying to achieve with my website is a very simple, minimalistic but also artistic aesthetic. My character, Naledi is very simple but also very artistic.

She is into Afrofuturism art, so I feel as though it would be important for me to represent all those sides of her through the look and feel of the website.
